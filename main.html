Rules

The cards are shuffled and laid on the table, face down. You should turn over two cards (one by one). If the images on the cards do not match, those cards are turned face down again. You will win when all the pairs have been found.

Press "Background" to choose background image.

Press "Difficulty" to choose the number of cards.

Press "Card Back" to choose card back image.

Good Luck!
